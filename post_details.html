Plage Achakar

900 views 1 comment April 20, 2026

A wild, untouched beach near the Caves of Hercules — one of Tangier's best-kept secrets.

Tucked away on the Atlantic side of the Cap Spartel peninsula, Plage Achakar is a raw and majestic stretch of coast beloved by surfers and adventurers. The waves here are powerful, and the scenery is breathtaking.

The beach is relatively undeveloped, which means you get an authentic experience away from the tourist crowds. Bring your own supplies and enjoy one of Morocco's most beautiful coastlines in peace.

Location

Achakar, Cap Spartel, Tangier
